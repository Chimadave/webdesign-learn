UFARM Services

UFARM activities includes the following:

  • Farm Set Up:For land owners (you) who desire to run a farm, but haven’t quite gotten around starting it up.
    1. You register on the UFARM platform, and provide details of the land you want to farm with (Image, size, location, legal proof and protection, what you want to farm etc).
    2. We carry on from there:
      • Make a feasibility study to corroborate or dismiss what you want to farm on the land.
      • Estimate how much is needed to set up the farm
      • Get investors for the project
      • Set up the farm.

Bearing the risks, UFARM and Investors collect 80% (this figure is subject to change) of the farm profits while running the farm for an agreed period while you enjoy 20% until hand-over where UFARM backs out and gives 80% of the farm to you after training you and your team to ensure you can continue well, while retaining 20% throughout the farm’s lifetime while providing consultancy services only to ensure you stay in business. Period before hand over is subject to business analysis and hence will be project dependent.

  • Farm/Land Management:Here, you don’t really care about farming, you just want the land provide returns for you instead of lying fallow and waiting for when you are ready to flip it or construct on it.
    1. You register on the UFARM platform, and provide details of the land you want to farm with (Image, size, location, legal proof and protection, what you want to farm etc).
    2. We carry on from there:
      • UFARM make feasibility studies and have a go/No-go decision (What is best to farm there, will it be profitable or not?)
      • Estimate how much investment is needed and gets the investors
      • UFARM obtains the right to farm on it from you for a given period after which we must roll up and leave the land as it was (just land). Lease can be renewed with you.
      • Set up a farm.
      • UFARM carries out the farming business and remits 30% of profits to you, from start to end of the relationship.

    I’m the founder of UFARM (Upfield Food And Resources Management) Nig Ltd a management company, that is focused on land use for food provision across the country.
    UFARM – Even a King depends on the harvest (Ecclesiastes 5:9)
    UFARM has just one activity: FARMING for YOU. You, being the land owners, we approach land owners to obtain a right to use their “un-used” piece of land that would otherwise be lying fallow for agricultural purposes while we share with them in the profits under pre-agreed terms.

    In 2014, we tested this idea with a plantain plantation in the south east region of Nigeria with significant response in willingness to “lease” out the land to us to farm but faced limitations from other business operations issues (harvest and sales basically), however we managed to keep the land owners happy at our loss. That was testing the waters for us. We still believe in the idea.
    Now, we have evolved, UFARM is incorporated, and is under way in producing her web platforms and brand identity for public communication. A business model has been put in place and a structure with professional legal support has been set up also. So we are going back in business.
